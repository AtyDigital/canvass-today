Canvass Scripts

Messaging frameworks for your field team

Standard Door Knock

Active
Opening
"Hi, my name is [YOUR NAME] and I’m volunteering for the Democratic Progress Party in the upcoming by-election. Do you have a moment to talk about the election?"
If YES — Issue Framing
"We’re focused on three key issues in Johannesburg East: reliable service delivery, safer streets, and economic opportunities for young people. How do you feel about those priorities in your ward?"
Closing
"Can we count on your support on election day? [Record response]. Thank you for your time — if you have any questions, here’s a leaflet with our candidate’s contact details."

Phone Bank Script

Draft
Opening
"Good [morning/afternoon], may I speak with [VOTER NAME]? Hi [NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] calling on behalf of the Democratic Progress Party. I’m reaching out to registered voters in Ward [X] about the upcoming by-election. Is now a good time?"
Key Question
"On a scale of 1 to 5, how likely are you to vote in the by-election? [Record answer]. And do you know where your nearest polling station is?"

Undecided Voter Persuasion

Review Needed
"I understand you’re still deciding — that’s completely fair. Can I share what our candidate has achieved in similar wards?"

Supporter Mobilisation

Active
"Great to hear you’re supporting us! Would you be willing to remind 3 neighbours to vote on election day? We’ll send you a reminder card you can share."
